Our Legacy

Strategically headquartered in the vibrant coastal hub of Negombo, ABC Travel has served as a distinguished pioneer in the Sri Lankan tourism industry for over 15 years. We take immense pride in curating bespoke, authentic travel experiences that bridge the gap between our guests and the rich cultural heritage, breathtaking natural landscapes, and the warm-hearted people of our island nation.


Our commitment is centered on delivering seamless, secure, and profoundly enriching travel solutions. Whether catering to adventurous local explorers or discerning international travelers, our mission remains steadfast: to unveil the true essence of Sri Lanka through unparalleled service and local expertise.
